# Instructions for Using the Website

Welcome to the dr-nandish-urologist-belagavi.github.io website! Below are the instructions on how to use this website effectively and link it to your Google Business Profile.

## Using the Website

1. **Home Page**: The home page provides an overview of the services offered. Browse through the sections to find the information you need.
2. **Contact Information**: Visit the contact page for details on how to reach out for appointments or inquiries.
3. **Service Listings**: Check the services section for detailed descriptions of what is offered.
4. **FAQs**: For common questions, refer to the FAQ section for quick answers.

## Linking to Google Business Profile

To link this website to your Google Business Profile, follow these steps:

1. **Log in to Google My Business**: Go to [Google My Business](https://www.google.com/business/) and log in with your account.
2. **Select Your Business**: Click on your business listing.
3. **Edit Website URL**: In the Info section, find the 'Website' field, and enter the URL of this GitHub Pages website (e.g., https://dr-nandish-urologist-belagavi.github.io).
4. **Save Changes**: Make sure to save your changes.

Now, customers can easily find and access your website directly from your Google Business Profile! 

Thank you for using our services!
